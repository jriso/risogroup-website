Top 10 BI Tools by Adoption: January 2026

Key Takeaways

  • Looker (Google) and Tableau (Salesforce) lead at ~68% adoption among high-growth tech companies, with Power BI (Microsoft) at 41%
  • Four VC-backed tools have reached 10%+ adoption: Metabase (15%), Hex (13%), Sigma (13%), and Mode (13%)
  • Hex and Sigma are tied at 13% in a competitive race, while Omni (7%) trails but is 2 years younger
  • Metabase leads the challengers at 15%, benefiting from a free open source tier
  • Streamlit (3%) ranks #10 with growth potential as AI agents can already easily generate Python-based dashboards programmatically

Methodology

Analysis of ~52,000 job descriptions from 1,097 VC-backed and public tech companies, scraped weekly from Dec 2025 ‚Äì Jan 2026. Companies categorized by business model. Shows % of companies mentioning each tool in at least one job description. 22 tools analyzed: Looker, Tableau, Power BI, Metabase, Hex, Sigma, Mode, Omni, Alteryx, Superset, Streamlit, Qlik, Domo, QuickSight, Sisense, ThoughtSpot, Redash, Lightdash, Cognos, MicroStrategy, Zenlytic, Deepnote.
