PostHog is eating product analytics

Key Takeaways

  • PostHog grew from 9% to 39% adoption in product analytics / CDP / event infrastructure software over 24 months, per Ramp spend data
  • 61% of first-time category buyers choose PostHog ‚Äî more than 6x the next closest competitor
  • PostHog over-indexes among SMBs at 45% adoption vs 39% overall, while legacy tools like Segment (23% vs 23%) and Amplitude (16% vs 17%) show flat or declining SMB share
  • On Google Trends, PostHog surpassed Mixpanel and Segment in search volume during 2025 and now rivals Amplitude, a public company spending $48M/quarter on sales and marketing

Methodology

Data sourced from Ramp's vendor directory as of February 2026. Ramp analyzes anonymized, aggregated transactions from businesses on its platform over a trailing 12-month window. Adoption rate measures the share of businesses that purchased from a vendor out of all businesses that made purchases in that category.
