NicheMerch

A live e-commerce store used as a testing ground for data and AI

nichemerch.shop

Overview

NicheMerch is a live Shopify store with over 10,000 standard items and the ability to generate custom products for individual customers or in batch. It serves as a testing ground for data and AI ideas with real customers and real transactions.

What's Under the Hood

  • Shopify storefront with 10,000+ SKUs
  • HatGPT: LLM-powered chat for custom merchandise design
  • Snowflake data warehouse
  • Agentic analytics pipeline
  • Paid marketing optimization
